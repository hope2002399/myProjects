Information we collect and how we use it: We offer a set of value-added services that require you to register for an account and provide any personal information to us, such as ScholarMate registration and subscription. Therefore, we may collect the following types of information:

1） Information you provide - When you sign up for a ScholarMate Account or other ScholarMate service or promotion that requires registration or subscription, we ask you for personal information (such as your name, email address and account password) in order to enable you to use the ScholarMate services. For certain services, such as our services subscription or advertising programs, we also request for credit card or other payment account information which we keep in encrypted form onto secure servers. We may combine those information you submit under your account with information made available from other ScholarMate services or third parties in order to better serve your needs and to improve the quality of our services. For certain services, we may give you the opportunity to opt out such act of combining the information.

2） ScholarMate cookies - Like most websites, ScholarMate uses cookies and web log files to track site usage and trends, to improve the quality of our service, to customize your experience on ScholarMate. When you visit ScholarMate, we send one or more cookies - a small file containing a string of characters - onto your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to help for improving the quality of our service to our valuable customers by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, for example, how users search their own or other scholarly work. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some ScholarMate features and services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled. One type of cookie, known as a “persistent” cookie, is set once you’ve logged in to your ScholarMate account. The next time you visit the ScholarMate website, the persistent cookie will allow us to recognize you as an existing User so you will not need to log in before using the Services. In order to access or change any of your private information (like a credit card number) or to send a message to another User, however, you must log in securely again with your password even if your computer contains your persistent cookie. Another type of cookie, called a “session” cookie, is used to identify a particular visit to the ScholarMate website. Session cookies expire after a short time or when you close your web browser.

3） Login information - When you use ScholarMate services, our servers automatically record information about what your browser sends whenever you visit a website. Our server may take logs on those information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

4） User communications - In order to respond to your requests, process your inquiries and enhance our services for you, we may keep record of the communications when you send email or other types of communications to ScholarMate.

5） Affiliated sites - As we may offer some of our services in conjunction with other web sites, the personal information provided by you to those sites may also be sent to ScholarMate in order to deliver the service. We process such information in according to this Policy. The affiliated sites may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies for information.

6） Other sites - This Privacy Policy applies to ScholarMate web sites and services that are owned and operated by IRIS Systems Limited. We do not exercise control over those sites displayed as search results or links from within our various services. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you. We encourage you to read their privacy policies for information as well.

7) Log files, IP addresses and information of your computer - Due to the communications standards on the internet, when you visit the ScholarMate website we automatically receive the URL of the site from which you came and the site to which you are going when you leave ScholarMate. ScholarMate also receives the internet protocol (“IP”) address of your computer (or the proxy server you use to access the World Wide Web), your computer operating system and type of web browser you are using, email patterns, mobile device operating system (if you are accessing LinkedIn using a mobile device), as well as the name of your ISP or your mobile carrier. The ScholarMate between your IP address and your personally identifiable information is not shared with third parties without your permission, except as the Compliance with Legal Process.
