About

ScholarMate is a research social network platform that connects people with knowledge (e.g. projects, publications and patents) to make innovation simple.

ScholarMate supports researchers to apply for funding and manage research projects with major funding agencies and research institutions. It also enables knowledge transfer (e.g. patent trading) by connecting researchers with innovators and entrepreneurs to share and discover knowledge.
