What is ScholarMate

Launched in 2006, ScholarMate is committed to connect people with knowledge to make innovation simple.

ScholarMate is a research social network that allows researchers to create profiles, and build connections with research collaborators to discover unknown opportunities.

ScholarMate is a research social media that allows researchers to upload and share publications so as to increase research visibilities and citations.

ScholarMate is a research eco-system that allows researchers to share and discover knowledge (e.g. publications, patents, projects, research grants, researchers, and research organizations), it is to promote research collaboration, academic exchange and knowledge sharing.
